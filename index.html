How it Works

Pick a store, shop & place your order

Our easy to use, intuitive mobile app makes your online shopping experience seamless and effortless. We are always looking to add more stores to our catalog. So, if you don't see your favorite store, don't worry, we are working on it!

We'll find a friendly neighbor to deliver

The smartcart app will match your orders with neighbors who might already be shopping at the same stores or are about to go shopping soon.
